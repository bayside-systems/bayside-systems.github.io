## Collaborative Secure Enclaves with Batteries Included

Xones is a secure, collaborative environment where humans and AI agents can work together seamlessly. It's not just a workspace — it's a complete operating environment with security, compliance, and governance built in from the ground up.

### The Problem

Modern AI systems need safe spaces to operate. Traditional development environments and collaboration tools weren't designed for human-agent teamwork. Data flows aren't controlled. Agents lack the context they need to operate effectively. Compliance requirements are difficult to maintain across distributed teams.

### The Xones Solution

Xones provides:

- **Secure Isolation**: All agent activity happens within a controlled, auditable environment
- **Human-Agent Collaboration**: Tools designed for people and AI to work together, not just for people
- **Built-in Governance**: Compliance, audit trails, and access control are native features, not add-ons
- **Full Stack**: Everything you need is included — no patching together disparate tools

### Use Cases

**Defense Research**  
Secure environments for classified or sensitive research where agents assist with analysis while maintaining strict data boundaries.

**Enterprise AI Deployment**  
Deploy AI agents that can collaborate with teams without exposing sensitive data or violating compliance requirements.

**AI Development & Testing**  
Create controlled environments for testing AI behavior, gathering telemetry, and ensuring safe agent operations.

### Technical Overview

Xones runs as a secure enclave — either locally or hosted — providing:

- Containerized execution environments
- Data flow controls and monitoring
- Role-based access for humans and agents
- Audit logging and compliance reporting
- API integrations with existing infrastructure
