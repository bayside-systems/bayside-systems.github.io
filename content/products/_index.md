Bayside Systems builds products for the next generation of AI infrastructure. Each product solves a specific problem in the AI ecosystem — from secure collaboration to identity verification.

## Product Lineup

### [Xones](/products/xones)
**Collaborative Secure Enclaves**  
A "batteries included" environment where humans and AI agents collaborate safely. Built-in security, compliance, and governance for agent-human teamwork.

### [PartiXen](/products/partixen)
**Operating System for Agent Ecosystems**  
Control what data enters an AI's context and what exits. The secure OS for managing data flow in and out of AI models.

### [undentity](/products/undentity)
**Disposable Identity Platform**  
Prove you're human without revealing who you are. 30-day identities for pseudo-anonymous online participation.

## Our Approach

All our products share a philosophy: **local-first, control-first**. Your data stays on your hardware unless you explicitly choose cloud hosting. We build for organizations that want AI capabilities without surrendering control.
