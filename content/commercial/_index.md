## Engagement Models

We offer flexible engagement structures to match your project needs and budget.

### Time & Materials (T&M)

**Best for:** Ongoing development, advisory engagements, or work where scope may evolve.

- **Billing:** Actual hours worked × applicable hourly rate
- **Minimum:** 20 hours/month
- **Invoicing:** Monthly, issued on the 1st for the prior month's work
- **Payment Terms:** Net 15 from invoice date
- **Hours Tracking:** Detailed weekly time reports with each invoice
- **Overtime:** Hours exceeding 160/month billed at 1.25× standard rate
- **Rate Lock:** Rates locked for the duration of the SOW (up to 12 months)
- **Termination:** 14 calendar days written notice by either party

### Fixed Firm Price (FFP)

**Best for:** Well-defined deliverables with clear acceptance criteria — proof of concepts, assessments, defined R&D tasks.

- **Billing:** Total fixed price for defined scope and deliverables
- **Payment Schedule:**
  - 25% upon SOW execution (non-refundable deposit)
  - 25% upon midpoint milestone delivery
  - 25% upon final deliverable delivery
  - 25% upon client written acceptance (Net 10)
- **Acceptance Period:** 10 business days to accept or object
- **Change Orders:** Required for any scope changes before work begins

### Monthly Retainer

**Best for:** Ongoing advisory, security monitoring, fractional CTO/CISO, or guaranteed availability needs.

- **Billing:** Fixed monthly fee for reserved hours
- **Standard Tiers:** 40 hrs/month | 80 hrs/month | 120 hrs/month
- **Pricing:** Hours × applicable rate, less 10% retainer discount
- **Invoicing:** Billed on the 1st of each month, in advance
- **Payment Terms:** Due upon receipt (prepaid)
- **Unused Hours:** Do not roll over to the following month
- **Overage Hours:** Billed at standard hourly rate on following invoice
- **Minimum Term:** 3 months initial commitment
- **Availability SLA:** Priority scheduling with guaranteed 4-hour response during business hours (M-F, 8AM-6PM ET)

### Project-Based Assessment (Flat Fee)

**Best for:** Security assessments, architecture reviews, compliance gap analyses.

| Assessment Type | Flat Fee | Duration | Deliverable |
|-----------------|----------|----------|-------------|
| CMMC/NIST 800-171 Gap Assessment | $12,500 | 2–3 weeks | Gap analysis report with remediation roadmap |
| Cloud Security Architecture Review | $15,000 | 2–3 weeks | Architecture findings, risk matrix, recommendations |
| AI/ML Model Risk Assessment | $18,000 | 3–4 weeks | Model evaluation report with bias analysis |
| Penetration Test (External + Internal) | $20,000 | 3–4 weeks | Detailed findings with severity ratings |
| Technical Due Diligence | $25,000+ | 2–4 weeks | Technical assessment report (scope-dependent) |

## Standard Commercial Terms

### Intellectual Property

- **Work-for-hire (default):** All deliverables created under the SOW are owned by the client upon full payment.
- **Pre-existing IP:** Tools, libraries, frameworks, or methodologies that existed prior to the engagement remain Bayside Systems' property. A perpetual, non-exclusive license to use pre-existing IP as incorporated into deliverables is granted to the client at no additional cost.
- **Residual Knowledge:** Nothing restricts Bayside Systems from using general knowledge, skills, and experience gained during the engagement, provided no client confidential information or proprietary data is disclosed.

### Confidentiality

- Mutual NDA executed prior to engagement or as part of the SOW
- Standard term: 3 years from disclosure
- Bayside Systems maintains information security practices aligned with NIST 800-171 and is prepared to handle CUI where contractually required

### Liability

- Total aggregate liability capped at total fees paid under the applicable SOW in the 12 months preceding the claim
- Neither party is liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, or punitive damages
- Bayside Systems maintains professional errors & omissions (E&O) insurance and cyber liability insurance

### Late Payment

- Invoices not paid within specified terms accrue interest at 1.5% per month (18% APR) on the outstanding balance
- Bayside Systems reserves the right to suspend work if any invoice remains unpaid for more than 30 days past due

### Governing Law

- Commonwealth of Virginia
- Dispute resolution via good faith negotiation followed by binding arbitration in Norfolk, Virginia

## Engagement Process

**Step 1 — Discovery Call** (no charge)  
Initial conversation to understand the problem, scope, and timeline. Typically 30–60 minutes.

**Step 2 — Scoping & Proposal** (no charge for standard engagements)  
Bayside Systems provides a written proposal or SOW within 5 business days of the discovery call, including scope, deliverables, pricing model, estimated timeline, and applicable terms.

**Step 3 — SOW Execution**  
Both parties sign the SOW. For FFP and retainer engagements, the initial payment is due before work begins.

**Step 4 — Kickoff & Delivery**  
Work commences per the agreed schedule. Regular status updates provided weekly or as defined in the SOW.

**Step 5 — Acceptance & Closeout**  
Deliverables reviewed per acceptance criteria. Final invoice issued upon completion.
