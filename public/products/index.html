Products

Tools and software we build, use, and share.

Our Approach

All our products share a philosophy: local-first, control-first. Your data stays on your hardware unless you explicitly choose cloud hosting. We build for organizations that want AI capabilities without surrendering control.

Defense & Government

All products support defense and government use cases:

  • Compliance with security requirements (FedRAMP, NIST, etc.)
  • Operational security built into product design
