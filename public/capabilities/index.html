Capabilities

AI/ML, automation pipelines, and defense research capabilities

Bayside Systems builds the infrastructure that powers the next generation of intelligent computing. From secure AI enclaves to identity verification, we engineer systems that are reliable, controllable, and built for real-world deployment.

AI & Machine Learning

We design and deploy production-grade AI systems that run where you need them — on premise, at the edge, or in the cloud. Our focus is on reliability and control, ensuring your AI pipelines are secure, auditable, and optimized for your specific workloads.

What we do:

  • Reliable AI Pipelines: Build end-to-end automation pipelines that handle data ingestion, model training, inference, and monitoring with minimal downtime and full traceability.
  • Model Tuning & Customization: Fine-tune foundation models to your domain, your data, and your performance requirements. We optimize for accuracy, latency, and resource efficiency.
  • Local Model Sizing: Right-size models to your compute environment. Whether you’re running on a local GPU cluster or embedded systems, we ensure your AI delivers results without overprovisioning infrastructure.
  • Local-First Architecture: Your data stays local by default. We offer hosted services for organizations that prefer managed operations, but our philosophy is built around local-first control.

Secure Agent Infrastructure

The era of autonomous agents is here. We’re building the secure operating environments that let people and AI agents collaborate safely.

Xones — Collaborative Secure Enclaves
Xones provides a secure environment where humans and agents work together with built-in security, compliance, and governance. Think of it as a “batteries included” workspace for agent-human collaboration.

PartiXen — Secure Operating System for Agent Ecosystems
If you need to control what data enters an AI’s context window — and strictly manage what exits through model outputs — PartiXen is the OS purpose-built for that control. It’s the secure foundation for agentic AI deployments.

Identity & Verification

undentity — Disposable Identity Platform
Prove you’re human without revealing who you are. undentity provides 30-day online identities that confirm you’re a real person in a geographic region — nothing more. Grab an identity at your local grocery store or Redbox, and use it for platforms where human pseudo-anonymity is the goal.
