Our Approach

We are a small firm by design. That means every person on the team is a practitioner, every project gets senior attention, and we do not sell work we cannot do ourselves.

Partnerships

We collaborate closely with clients, prime contractors, and academic institutions to deliver solutions that work in practice, not just on paper. Our best work comes from long-term relationships built on trust and shared technical goals.

Technical Depth

Our team brings deep expertise across the full stack — from low-level systems engineering to applied research — with a focus on practical, deployable results. We write code, run experiments, and ship working systems.

Every engagement is oriented toward measurable outcomes. We prioritize impact over overhead, and substance over presentation. If something does not need to be complex, we do not make it complex.
